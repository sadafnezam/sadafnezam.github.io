Debt Rush

Spring 2026 Capstone project 3-member team Unity

A co-op top-down action game where two partners manage a shared budget to survive the debt collectors and escape the city.

My roleDeveloper - Designer - UX Research

What I did
  • Implemented local multiplayer input with per-device control schemes via the Input System, supporting both keyboard/mouse and gamepads.
  • Built the shop and inventory UI with Unity UI Toolkit (UXML/USS/C#), and designed the menu and HUD layouts.
  • Designed and implemented the weapon system and mechanics, including equip, aim, fire, and collision detection.
  • Implemented enemy AI behaviors including movement constraints, target detection, and state handling.
  • Designed the city map with distinct urban and suburban regions, placing enemies, NPCs, and loot, and using the suburban area as the tutorial space.
  • Conducted playtesting sessions with usability surveys, and used the findings to improve UI feedback and gameplay clarity.

The Up-Grader

Spring 2025 Course project 5-member team Unity

A 2D Metroidvania platformer where you play as a broken little robot looking for its missing pieces to build itself back together, but every upgrade comes at a cost.

My roleGameplay Programmer - System Designer - Level Designer

What I did
  • Programmed core gameplay systems using Unity Visual Scripting, including combat logic, enemy behavior, player movement, abilities, and progression.
  • Implemented ranged enemy AI with chase, positioning, attack, and leash-back behavior.
  • Designed and implemented tiered enemy types with escalating speed, damage, fire rate, and increasingly complex shooting patterns.
  • Designed loot and enemy systems, defining drop tables and stat differences across enemy and weapon types.
  • Implemented the checkpoint system with player death and respawn logic.
  • Designed the level layout, from blockout to tile painting, and placement of enemies, resources, and checkpoints across the map.
